About EduAcademy

EduAcademy was founded in 2015 to provide affordable and accessible education to learners in underserved communities in South Africa.

Our Mission

To empower learners through quality education, tutoring, and digital resources.

Our Vision

A future where every learner has equal opportunities to succeed academically and personally.
